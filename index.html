Mechanical and Software Techie

I find great passion for learning and take on challenging issues involving the automotive & aerospace industry, mobility industry, renewable technology and child care industry head on. I love to be involved in a hyper driven team where I can develop and contribute my skills with teams who are passionate in their crafts and who are eager to work and play as a team.
